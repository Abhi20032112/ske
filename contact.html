Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

What types of organizations do you serve?

We serve government departments, PSUs, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and corporate organizations across India. Our solutions are specifically designed for institutional and bulk procurement needs.

How do I get a quotation for bulk orders?

You can request a quotation by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or sending an email with your requirements. Please include product specifications, quantities, delivery timeline, and location details for accurate pricing.

Do you provide installation and training services?

Yes, we provide complete end-to-end services including product delivery, professional installation, user training, and ongoing technical support. Our team ensures smooth implementation and operation of all solutions.

What is your typical delivery timeline?

Delivery timelines vary based on product type, quantity, and location. Standard products typically take 2-4 weeks, while custom solutions may require 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the quotation process.

Do you offer warranty and after-sales support?

All our products come with manufacturer warranties and our dedicated after-sales support. We provide ongoing maintenance services, technical support, and replacement parts as needed to ensure optimal performance.

Can you handle multi-location deployments?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience in managing large-scale, multi-location deployments across different states. Our logistics team coordinates deliveries and installations to ensure synchronized implementation.
